About Me

Since graduating from Roberto-Venn School of Luthiery in 1992, I have been obsessed with with doing the very best guitar repairs I possibly can. Nothing is better than giving an out of whack or damaged guitar a new lease on life. I've always believed a well set-up instrument is far more inspiring to play on.

Services

I can take care of just about any stringed instrument repair you might need, as well as some other types. Anything from simple repairs like jack replacement to neck resets and refrets, as well as finishing work. Custom modifications of all kinds as well! Authorized Buzz Feiten retrofitter. I'll call you with an estimate once I've seen your guitar, and work will not begin without your approval.
